Inspired by the pencil-and-paper game of many names, Dots & Lines is a strategy game where you must place lines, joining adjacent dots, to complete boxes. Being the last to place a box's fourth line earns you that box. Play the classic version, a power-up infused battle version, or take on the campaign mode with varying board layouts.
